# Solenhive Brand Guide
**Strategy built to scale**

---

## 1. The Name

**Solenhive** is a compound of two meaningful roots:

- **Sol** — Latin for sun. Light, clarity, warmth. Native to Florida.
- **Hive** — collective intelligence, organized systems working toward a common goal.

Together: a sun-powered hive. Intelligent systems, strategic clarity, built in Florida.

The name sits above everything — consulting, SaaS products, and ventures — as the parent brand.

---

## 2. Logo

### The Mark
The honeycomb circle replaces the "o" in **solen**, integrating the icon directly into the wordmark. The mark consists of:
- A navy circle container
- Seven hexagonal cells tiled in a honeycomb pattern (gold outlines)
- One filled gold center cell — the focal point, representing the sun and the queen cell

### Lockup Versions
| Version | Use |
|---|---|
| Full lockup (wordmark + tagline) | Website headers, documents, presentations |
| Wordmark only (no tagline) | Email signatures, letterheads |
| Icon only (circle mark) | Favicon, app icon, social avatar |

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the lowercase "o" on all sides of the lockup.

### Don't
- Don't stretch or distort the mark
- Don't change the font weight
- Don't use the icon on a background that reduces contrast below 4.5:1
- Don't rearrange the wordmark elements

## 4. Typography

### Typeface
**Gill Sans** (system) — geometric sans-serif, light weight.

Fallback stack: `'Gill Sans', 'Gill Sans MT', 'Trebuchet MS', Calibri, sans-serif`

### Scale
| Role | Size | Weight | Tracking |
|---|---|---|---|
| Wordmark | 40px | 300 | 2px |
| H1 Hero | 56–72px | 300 | -0.5px |
| H2 Section | 36px | 300 | 0 |
| H3 Card title | 20px | 400 | 0 |
| Body | 16px | 300 | 0 |
| Tagline / label | 9.5px | 300 | 3.5px |
| Nav | 12px | 400 | 2px |

### Rules
- Wordmark and hero headlines always in weight 300 (light)
- All caps only for labels, taglines, and navigation — never for body copy
- Letter-spacing on all-caps text: minimum 2px, preferred 3–4px

## 5. Voice & Tone

**What Solenhive sounds like:**
- Confident without being loud
- Direct — no filler words
- Smart but not academic
- Outcomes-first — lead with what you deliver, not how you do it

**What Solenhive never sounds like:**
- Buzzword-heavy ("synergize", "disrupt", "leverage")
- Vague ("we help businesses grow")
- Overly casual (no slang)

**Example — wrong:**
> "We leverage cutting-edge digital strategies to disrupt the marketing space and deliver synergistic growth solutions."

**Example — right:**
> "We build marketing systems and digital products that perform. Strategy first, execution always."
